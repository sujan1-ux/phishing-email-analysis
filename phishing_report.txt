phishing_report.txtPhishing Indicators:

1. Sender Email:
The email address "support@amaz0n-security.com" is suspicious because it mimics a real company but uses a fake domain.

2. Suspicious Link:
The link "http://amaz0n-login-security.com" is not an official Amazon website.

3. Urgent Language:
The email creates panic by saying the account will be suspended within 24 hours.

4. Spoofing:
The sender pretends to be Amazon but is not legitimate.

5. Grammar Issues:
The message contains unnatural wording and lacks professionalism.

Conclusion:
This email is a phishing attempt designed to steal user credentials.
